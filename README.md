## What It Does

Minecraft Utilities delivers a collection of browser-based helpers that let you manage your Minecraft experience without leaving your browser. Whether you are laying out complex redstone circuits, calculating material needs for a major construction, or documenting your exploration journey, these tools provide a streamlined interface for planning and organizing.

The suite operates on the principle that planning should happen separately from playing. Rather than juggling spreadsheets or external applications, you get a dedicated workspace to test block counts, examine material ratios, and store your configurations for future sessions. All processing occurs client-side, meaning your data stays on your machine unless you choose to export it.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I begin using the tools?**  
Open the `index.html` file in your browser. The main menu displays all available tools. Click any tool to access its interface.

**Is my data preserved between sessions?**  
Yes. The tools rely on your browser's local storage to keep configurations, project data, and preferences. Clearing your browser cache will erase this information.

**Can I modify the tools to suit my needs?**  
Each tool includes a settings panel where you can adjust calculation methods, display preferences, and export options. No programming knowledge is required.

**Does this work on mobile devices?**  
The interface adapts to tablets and phones, although certain tools like the seed map viewer perform best on larger screens.

**Where does my data reside?**  
All data stays in your browser's local storage. No information is transmitted to any server. You can export your data as a file for safekeeping.

**How do I upgrade to a newer version?**  
Download the latest build and replace your existing folder with the new one. Your saved data will remain intact as long as you use the same browser.
